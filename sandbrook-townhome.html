- Sandbrook Townhome #14 -

Whiteface Club & Resort

Beautifully maintained Resort townhome overlooking the 18th fairway at Whiteface Club Resort on the shore of Lake Placid Lake.

Features include a main entry way with hall closet and utility/pantry room leading into the main living area, dining and kitchen. Kitchen was recently updated with Corian tile counter tops, hardwood floor, new light fixtures and appliances. Dining room offers good views of the mountains and golf course along with sliding glass doors leading to a deck and lawn area. A spacious master bedroom with private full bath is also included on the first floor.

An open stairway leads to a small open balcony with two additional bedrooms. A second master bedroom with totally remodeled private bath and a guest bedroom with a shared full bath.

The lower level with it's own separate entrance through the garage, contains a large bedroom, rec room, ski tuning or other activities room and full bath. This area could be a great space for the kids.

Attached 1.5 car garage with a small storage area and places to hang you skis and equipment.

Sandbrook owners have the use of all resort amenities through their membership which is included in the monthly common fees for the HOA along with all exterior grounds maintenance, lawns, gardens, snow plowing and shoveling. Included is free golf, tennis, swimming beach, boat slip(additional), cross country skiing and two restaurants.
