- Cervo Lodge -

Lake Placid Residential

Cervo Lodge in the Village of Lake Placid, consists 5 bedrooms and 3 full baths.

Cervo can comfortably accommodate up to 10-12 guest in beds making it the perfect property for large families, Corporate groups or Wedding parties.

An open floor plan and fully equipped kitchen featuring a dishwasher, stainless steel appliances, refrigerator, microwave and stove/oven; ample cupboards for storage. Stocked with pots/pans/dishes/glassware/utensils/dishtowels/limited spices/initial supply of paper towels, toilet tissue, etc.

Cervo features a comfortable living area, smart TV's and WiFi. In addition the lower level offers a second TV/rec room with futon sofa bed, two guest bedrooms, full bath and utility room.

Cervo is located within a 5 minute drive or shuttle bus ride to the Olympic Center, Mirror Lake and public swimming, shops, restaurants, groceries and pharmacies, hair salons, spas, a fitness center, and family entertainment centers.

The free Trolley, which stops directly below the Lodge, will take you anywhere around town, and the Whiteface Ski shuttle bus that takes you directly to Whiteface Mountain.

The Adirondacks offer so many opportunities to experience the great outdoors. Numerous nearby lakes and rivers provide our guests with swimming, rafting, boating, kayaking, SUP, canoeing, and fishing. The mountains offer hiking, rock climbing, ice climbing, and of course, winter skiing. Additionally, there are opportunities for horseback riding, snow shoeing, cross country skiing and summer hiking and camping. There are many guide services and ski shops that can help you plan your outdoor events and can rent you all the equipment you need.

There is also plenty to do if you are not the "outdoorsy" type: stores, gift shops, theaters, art galleries, book stores, yarn shops, farm markets, Olympic Museums, and stunning road trip opportunities.

Inside Cervo Lodge you will find a notebook that will offer advice on hiking, restaurants along with checking out instructions. Please feel free to call me any time with questions or suggestions.
