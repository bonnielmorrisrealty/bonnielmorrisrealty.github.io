Whiteface Club & Resort

Gorgeous three bedrooms(one offering lake & mountain views), two bath lake front condominium on the shore of Lake Placid lake. Beautifully renovated in rustic Adirondack style with whole log accents, twig railings and trim work, handcrafted furnishings and granite tile.

The open floor plan offers stunning lake and mountain views, wood burning fireplace, dining area, Chef's kitchen with stainless steel appliances and glass doors leading to an open balcony.

Whiteface Club & Resort on the shore of Lake Placid lake offers amenities for the whole family: swimming beach (beach passes included), full service Marina with boat rentals, slip rentals to bring your own boat and waterfront restaurant; 18 hole Championship golf, 6 clay Tennis courts, hiking and biking trails; clinics for all ages-sailing, kayaking, tennis & golf; Relax with Yoga on the waterfront;

Winter brings (equipment rentals available) cross country skiing on groomed trails through the 400 scenic acres; back country skiing on the JackRabbit trail system extending miles through the mountains; snowshoeing; ice skating and tubing; walking distance to two restaurants including the famous Lake Placid Lodge for fine dining or pub dining;

The resort is a short drive to the Olympic Village of Lake Placid for exploring the Olympic venues, shopping and restaurants; Ski or Mountain Bike Whiteface Mountain, 9 miles from the Village; Cross Country Ski at the Olympic Complex/Mt. Vanhoevenberg; go for a Bobsled or Luge run; Ice Skate on the Olympic Oval; Lake Placid has it all.
