- Graceland Lodge -

Whiteface Club & Resort

Graceland Lodge is a custom built Adirondack Rustic style home situated on a private lot overlooking the golf course and mountains.

The home features an open floor plan that includes a great room with vaulted pine tongue and groove ceilings, floor to ceiling stone fireplace, kitchen with slate counter top and stainless steel appliances, french doors leading into a glassed sunroom with a second stone fireplace and twin lounge bed. The main floor also includes a master bedroom suite with master bath that includes a jetted tub, glassed shower and double sinks, walk in closet and sitting area.

Open log stairway leads to the second floor wrap around open loft, library area and two guest bedrooms, each with private baths.

The lower level features a rec area with table tennis, fourth bedroom that includes a bunk bed with one double, one twin and a trundle double bed and shared bathroom.

Lower level walks out to a brick patio and fire pit area surrounded by trees for privacy. Two car attached garage and parking for 6+ cars.

Whiteface Club & Resort on the shore of Lake Placid lake offers amenities for the whole family: swimming beach, full service Marina with boat rentals, slip rentals to bring your own boat and waterfront restaurant; 18 hole Championship golf, 6 clay Tennis courts, hiking and biking trails; clinics for all ages-sailing, kayaking, tennis & golf; Relax with Yoga on the waterfront; All amenities on a fee basis for guest.

Winter brings (equipment rentals available) cross country skiing on groomed trails through the 400 scenic acres; back country skiing on the Jack Rabbit trail system extending miles through the mountains; snowshoeing; ice skating and tubing; walking distance to two restaurants including the famous Lake Placid Lodge for fine dining or pub dining;

The resort is a short drive to the Olympic Village of Lake Placid for exploring the Olympic venues, shopping and restaurants; Ski or Mountain Bike Whiteface Mountain, 9 miles from the Village; Cross Country Ski at the Olympic Complex/Mt. Vanhoevenberg; go for a Bobsled or Luge run; Ice Skate on the Olympic Oval; Lake Placid has it all.
