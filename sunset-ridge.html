- Sunset Ridge -

Lake Placid Village

Welcome to Sunset Ridge in Lake Placid, New York. Sunset Ridge has been thoughtfully developed by the owner who has lived and owned the property along with his family for generations.

Situated on the edge of Lake Placid Village and adjoining the distinguished Whiteface Lodge Resort, Sunset Ridge offers many amenities for owners outside the development, such as membership and use of the resorts world class facilities; also nearby is the Whiteface Club & Resort with amenities for outside enjoyment such as their 18-hole Championship golf course, 6-Har Tru clay tennis courts, sandy swimming beach, marina and boat slips, all within a close drive or walk.

As you enter Sunset Ridge you will quickly recognize the importance the developer placed on theinterior improvements such as roadways, buried municipal electric & water lines and fire hydrants; each lot has an defined building envelope along with engineered perk test for easy installation of your private septic system;

The development will include a trail around Sunset Ridge giving lot owners a private trail systemleading around the perimeter of the development along with easy access to the adjoining Jackrabbit trail system for hiking, biking and cross country skiing.

There are a total of 19 lots to choose from that vary in size from .5 acre up to 5+ acres; most lots will offer views of the McKenzie Mountain range, all will offer privacy and a mature forest mixture of pines and some hardwoods; Surrounded by State owned lands, owners will enjoy the sounds of the Loon who nest in the wetland areas below the development; deer are plentiful along with an occasional black bear passing through, fox, coyote and a wild variety of birds. Sunset Ridge will give you the feeling of being in the Wilderness yet a close walk to two of the most popular resorts in the Adirondack Park and the Village of Lake Placid.

Total Lots: 19

Lot Sizes: 0.5 to 5 acres

List Price: $90,000 up to $279,000
