Lake Placid Vacation Rental

Morningside Townhomes are located in one of the best locations for easy access Main Street Lake Placid and Mirror Lake.

This immaculate Adirondack style home sleeps 8 guests with an open floor plan making it perfect for entertaining. The first floor consists of a fully equipped kitchen, dining area, a great room with vaulted ceilings, a gas fireplace, laundry room and a full bathroom with jetted tub.

The open stairway leads to the second floor which features a queen bedroom, a guest bedroom with two double beds, a third guest bedroom with a double bed and open balcony. A second shared bathroom is on the second floor.

Morningside #25 welcomes well-behaved pets for an additional $100 pet fee and gives many options for taking your furry friend for walks.

Within a close walk is the Lake Placid Golf Club, Mirror Lake with a public beach area, tennis courts, shops, restaurants, Olympic Center, Olympic Oval and many Olympic venues.

Whiteface Mountain is 9 miles from the Village with shuttle buses running every 45 minutes from Main Street. The Horse Show grounds and Olympic Ski Jumping Complex are a close 2 mile drive.

The Adirondacks offer so many opportunities to experience the great outdoors. Numerous nearby lakes and rivers provide our guests with swimming, rafting, boating, kayaking, SUP, canoeing, and fishing. The mountains offer hiking, rock climbing, ice climbing, and of course, winter skiing. Additionally, there are opportunities for horseback riding, snow shoeing, cross country skiing and summer hiking and camping. There are many guide services and ski shops that can help you plan your outdoor events and can rent you all the equipment you need. There is also plenty to do if you are not the "outdoorsy" type: stores, gift shops, theaters, art galleries, bookstores, yarn shops, farm markets, Olympic Museums, and stunning road trip opportunities.

Upon checking into your home, you will find your ‘Welcome’ notebook which offers useful information for your stay; emergency numbers, things to do & see, close hiking/skiing trails, recommended restaurants and shopping, along with ‘check out’ requirements.
