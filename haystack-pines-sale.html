- Haystack Pines -

Lake Placid Residential

Haystack Pines is a single family home situated in a premiere setting in Lake Placid. Located off the Whiteface Inn Lane and within a close walk to Whiteface Lodge and Whiteface Club Resort. The property consists of 3 wooded acres that are bordered by State owned lands with trails leading to the Jackrabbit trail system for hikiing, biking and cross country skiing.

The home features an open floor plan ideal for entertaining with living room, dining area and kitchen with a wood burning fireplace and entrance onto a huge wrap around deck.

A master bedroom features built in shelving and dressers, private master bath and picture windows overlooking the woods. A guest bedroom on the main floor includes a queen bed and twin bunks beds with a shared guest bath. The front sun room offers easy access to the deck along with peaceful winter mountain views through the trees.

The lower level includes a rec room with foos ball table, a third guest bedroom and guest bathroom. A center foyer mud room offers attached one car garages on either side along with a detached 2-3 car garage with carport.

Currently this home is a successful vacation rental with a strong rental history and bookings into 2018. Nightly rates vary from $325-$600 per night plus clean fees, taxes and deposit.

Being offered completely furnished, turn-key.
