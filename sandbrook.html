- Sandbrook Town Home #16 -

Whiteface Club & Resort

Sandbrook #16 is a one owner unit since it was built in 1985. Meticulously maintained, this end unit would be an excellent vacation home and/or seasonal rental unit;

The full finished basement includes a den, small guest bedroom, full bath and utilityroom; attached is a one car garage;

The lot is very private with woods and Sand Brook running through the back yard; two decks, one in the front of the unit that overlooks the golf course and the side deck overlooks the woods and stream;

The monthly common fee gives the owner two full memberships the Whiteface Club & Resort offering free golf, tennis, swimming, boat slips are available but are an additional fee, cross country skiing in the winter, three restaurants within walking distance and full exterior maintenance of your property;
