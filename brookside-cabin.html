Brookside Cabin

Whiteface Club & Resort

One queen bedroom with private full bath, jetted tub, glassed in stall shower; guest bedroom with two queen beds and private full bath; library with queen size pull out sofa; third bath off the kitchen; great room with stone fireplace-woodburning; dining area overlooking sand brook; screened in porch and open deck overlooking the mountains;

Whiteface Club & Resort on the shore of Lake Placid lake offers amenities for the whole family: swimming beach, full service Marina with boat rentals, slip rentals to bring your own boat and waterfront restaurant; 18 hole Championship golf, 6 clay Tennis courts, hiking and biking trails; clinics for all ages-sailing, kayaking, tennis & golf; Relax with Yoga on the waterfront;

Winter brings (equipment rentals available) cross country skiing on groomed trails through the 400 scenic acres; back country skiing on the Jack Rabbit trail system extending miles through the mountains; snowshoeing; ice skating and tubing; walking distance to two restaurants including the famous Lake Placid Lodge for fine dining or pub dining;

The resort is a short drive to the Olympic Village of Lake Placid for exploring the Olympic venues, shopping and restaurants; Ski or Mountain Bike Whiteface Mountain, 9 miles from the Village; Cross Country Ski at the Olympic Complex/Mt. Vanhoevenberg; go for a Bobsled or Luge run; Ice Skate on the Olympic Oval; Lake Placid has it all.
