Saranac Lake Vacation Rental

Stunning Waterfront Home 3 Bed / 3 Bath - Wonderful Setting Year-Around

This charming vacation rental is perched above Lower Saranac Lake with stunning views and ample opportunities for adventure. With close proximity to Saranac Lake, Lake Placid, and Whiteface Mountain you will never be bored. In the summer months take out a boat, kayak, paddleboard, or swim in one of the most beautiful lakes in the Adirondacks. In the winter, ski, skate, snowmobile, or sit back and relax in front of the fire.

This three-bed, three-bath townhouse is perfect for couples or families of 6. Each bedroom is equipped with its own rain shower and the primary bedroom has a huge jacuzzi tub overlooking the lake. You will have the opportunity to observe common loons, bald eagles, great blue herons, beaver, and even moose on the double balconies.

With Central A/C and heat you are guaranteed comfort year-round. You can entertain with a large dining room table, well-equipped kitchen, and grill on the deck. If going out is more your speed, the Village of Saranac Lake offers a variety of shopping, art galleries, fine dining, and small diners to choose from. The Olympic Village of Lake Placid is located 8 miles from the property.
