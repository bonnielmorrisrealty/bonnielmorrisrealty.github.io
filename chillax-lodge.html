- Chillax Lodge -

Whiteface Club Resort

Olympic Resort Condominiums were built in the late 1970’s to be used specifically for housing the competing athletes in the 1980 Winter Olympics. Chillax Lodge has been completely remodeled and features an open floor plan with one bedroom, one full bath, kitchen/dining area, living room with wood burning wood stove and covered balcony.

Whiteface Club & Resort on the shore of Lake Placid lake offers amenities on a fee basis for the whole family including a full-service Marina, two restaurants, an 18-hole Championship golf course, 6 clay Tennis courts, hiking and biking trails. Winter amenities include cross country skiing on groomed trails, back country skiing on the Jackrabbit trails, snowshoeing, ice skating and tubing.

The resort is a short two-mile drive to the Olympic Village of Lake Placid for exploring the Olympic venues, shopping and restaurants. Whiteface Mountain is nine miles from Chillax with down hill skiing and snowboarding from November through April and mountain biking during the summer months.

Several Cross-Country Ski areas are within a close drive from Lake Placid including the Olympic Complex/Mt. Vanhoevenberg where there are miles of groomed trails or you can take a Bobsled or Luge run on the original Olympic tracks. Ice skating can be experienced on the Olympic Oval, Mirror Lake or try the Cascade Lakes for a true Adirondack experience. Lake Placid truly has it all.

Pets are welcome.
