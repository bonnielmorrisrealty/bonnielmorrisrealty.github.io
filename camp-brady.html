- Camp Brady -

Whiteface Club & Resort

Camp Brady is an elegant Adirondack rustic style log home offering some of the best lake and mountain range views of any home available.

As soon as you enter Camp Brady you will feel the warmth of the Adirondacks with whole log open stairway, hardwood floors, birch bark and twig décor and built in’s, vaulted ceiling finishedin wood and granite counter tops; the first floor with its open floor plan is the perfect place for entertaining…great room with custom tiled fireplace, dining table for 10, massive handcrafted chandelier and Chef’s kitchen with Viking appliances and granite counter tops; Glass front and lots of windows giving a stunning lake and mountain views from most every room; In addition on the main level is a guest bedroom with two twin beds and shared full bath;

The open stairway leads to the open loft/family room with office work area and the master suite with king size bed, walk in closet and full bath with jetted tub, glasses shower, double sinks and surrounded by granite

The walk out lower level of the home consists of a family room with a second TV and a pull out sofa bed; Guest bedroom with queen bed and private full bath; a second guest bedroom with twotwin beds and a fourth bath; this level walks out to the back yard and a short walk to the beach/swimming area and marina.

Open deck off of the great/dining room along with an enclosed screened porch with stone fireplace offers a great place to enjoy the spectacular views.

Located at the Whiteface Club & Resort on the shore of Lake Placid lake this property gives guestpasses for use of the swimming beach; other amenities are also available on a fee basis;

Near the swimming beach is a full service Marina with boat rentals and slip’s available to bring your own boat; waterfront restaurant (open only in the spring/summer); 18 hole Championshipgolf course, 6 clay Tennis courts, hiking and biking trails plus the adjoining Jackrabbit Trail system; clinics for all ages-sailing, kayaking, tennis & golf; Relax with Yoga on the waterfront

Winter brings cross country skiing on groomed trails on the golf course through the 400 scenic acres(equipment rentals available); back country skiing on the Jackrabbit trail system extendingmiles through the mountains; snowshoeing; ice skating and tubing; walking distance to two restaurants including the famous Lake Placid Lodge for fine dining or pub dining; Whiteface Lodge within a close drive/walk with various dining options and spa services;

Camp Brady is a short drive to the Olympic Village of Lake Placid for exploring the Olympic venues, shopping and restaurants; Ski or Mountain Bike Whiteface Mountain depending on the time of year…9 miles from the Village with shuttle buses that run every 45 minutes; Cross Country Ski at the Olympic Complex/Mt. Vanhoevenberg; go for a Bobsled or Luge run onthe Olympic facility; Ice Skate on the Olympic Oval in the center of the Village; Hiking surrounds us with the 46 High Peaks along with thousand of shorter trails;Lake Placid has something to do in every season for the whole family.
