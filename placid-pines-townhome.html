Lake Placid Vacation Rental

Placid Pines is a spacious and well-equipped 4 bedroom, 3 bathrooms Townhome in Lake Placid within a close walk to Main Street shopping, restaurants, and venues.

The entry-level holds a queen bedroom, a full bathroom, laundry room, and a den.

The 2nd level has a queen bedroom and an open area that includes a kitchen, dining room, living room with gas fireplace, and a full bathroom.

The upper level holds a king bedroom, a queen bedroom, and another full bathroom.

All of the bedrooms, the den and living room are each equipped with brand new smart TVs for guests' enjoyment throughout the home.

A large covered screen porch offers a perfect spot to enjoy your morning coffee or to just sit and relax after a busy day of exploring and activities!

Take advantage of the Trolley Stop just steps away that will give you a free ride to local venues, shops, restaurants, visit the Olympic Arenas for shows and public skating or take a dip in Mirror Lake at the public beach with playground and tennis courts.

Well equipped and ready for your family reunion.
