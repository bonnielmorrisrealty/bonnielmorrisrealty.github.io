- Camp Bearadise -

Lake Placid Vacation Rental

Situated in the heart of Whiteface Club Resort. Camp Bearadise...Beautiful townhome that has been newly updated with new beds, linens, dishes, and décor. 4 bedrooms, 4 full baths. Great room with wood burning wood stove, wood supplied, fully equipped kitchen, dining for 8 with a kitchen/bar dining option. Each bedroom has a private bath, master bedroom includes a sauna to enjoy after a long day of hiking or skiing.

One king, one queen, one bunk room with twin over twin, lower level bedroom with a Queen bed and private bath.

Sleeps 8 in beds.

Camp Bearadise is located at Whiteface Club Resort with its gorgeous manicured grounds and gardens, marina for boat rentals, golf, tennis, and three restaurants...winter offers groomed cross country skiing, snowshoeing, and sledding. all amenities are on a fee basis.

Sit on your deck and enjoy the outdoor firepit and quiet setting.

Escape the craziness of downtown Lake Placid yet be only 5 minutes by car to the center of the Village. Enjoy the amenities of resort living including Golf, Tennis, a Marina, two Resort restaurants serving 7 days a week during the summer, and a 5-minute walk to the well-known Lake Placid Lodge with fine dining & Maggie's Pub on the lake for a more relaxed atmosphere.

Also included are high-speed WiFi, Two Smart TVs, a wood-burning fireplace with the wood included, propane grill on the deck.

Bringing a boat? No problem, the marina at the Club has available weekly rentals of docks as well as kayaks, canoes, SUP's and sailboats for rent, however, you will need to reserve your spot prior to your arrival as the slips fill up quickly.
