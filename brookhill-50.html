- Brookhill 50 -

Whiteface Club & Resort

Brookhill 50 is a totally renovated, upper floor condominium in Adirondack Rustic style offering fantastic Lake Placid lake and mountain views.

The home features a stone fireplace, kitchen with granite counter top and stainless steel appliances, dining area leading out to a covered balcony. The main floor features two bedrooms and two baths. A custom built spiral log stairway leads to the second floor loft/den area, a third bedroom and full bath with soaking tub and a second balcony/deck for enjoying the sun and views.

Membership to Whiteface Club & Resort is included in your monthly common fees and gives owners full use of all resort amenities including...use of the swimming beach, full service Marina with boat rentals, slip rentals, waterfront golf course restaurant, an 18 hole Championship golf, 6 clay Tennis courts, hiking and biking trails. Clinics for all age are offered on a fee basis for sailing, kayaking, tennis & golf.

Winter brings (equipment rentals available) cross country skiing on groomed trails through the 400 scenic acres, back country skiing on the Jack Rabbit trail system extending miles through the mountains, snowshoeing, ice skating and tubing. The unit is walking distance to three restaurants including the famous Lake Placid Lodge for fine dining or Maggie pub for casual dining.

The resort is a short drive to the Olympic Village of Lake Placid for exploring the Olympic venues, shopping and restaurants, skiing or mountain biking on Whiteface Mountain is just 9 miles from the Village. Cross Country Ski at the Olympic Complex/Mt. Vanhoevenberg or go for a Bobsled or Luge run. The whole family will enjoy ice skating on the Olympic Oval and tobogganing onto a frozen Mirror Lake. Lake Placid has it all.
