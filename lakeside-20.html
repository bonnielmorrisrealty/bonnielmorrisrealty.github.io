- Lakeside #20 -

Whiteface Club Resort

Direct lake access from this walk out level, two bedroom, two bath condominium on the shore of Lake Placid lake.

Features include a main entry way with hall closet leading into the main living area, dining and kitchen. Kitchen was recently updated with Cherry cabinets, granite counter tops, tile flooring and stainless steel appliances. Living area offers good views of the lake and mountains with sliding glass doors leading to the patio and lawn area. Living area contains a wood burning fireplace with accented Cherry in-laid ceiling. The master bathroom features a jacuzzi tub, stall shower, new sink with granite counter tops and washer/dryer unit.

Lakeside owners have the use of all resort amenities through their membership which is included in the monthly common fees for the HOA along with all exterior grounds maintenance, lawns, gardens, snow plowing and shoveling. Included is free golf, tennis, swimming beach, boat slip(additional), cross country skiing and two restaurants.
