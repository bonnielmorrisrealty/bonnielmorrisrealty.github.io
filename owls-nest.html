Owl's Nest Lodge - 13 Overlook

Whiteface Club & Resort

Custom built Adirondack home overlooking the lake and mountains at Whiteface Club & Resort on the shore of Lake Placid lake. Two levels of living that include two queen bedrooms, one with private master bath, jetted tub and glassed shower, open loft with two additional queen beds, shared full bath, fully equipped kitchen with granite counter tops, stone fireplace, hardwood floors and 14' vaulted ceilings; upper level sleeps up to 8; The lower walk out level features a second fully equipped kitchen with granite counter tops, hardwood floors, stone fireplace, full bath, one queen bedroom, a second bedroom with two queen beds; additional sleeping for up to 6;

The rate includes use of the resort amenities, including golf and tennis. Whiteface Club & Resort on the shore of Lake Placid lake offers amenities for the whole family: swimming beach, full service Marina with boat rentals, slip rentals to bring your own boat and waterfront restaurant; 18 hole Championship golf, 6 clay Tennis courts, hiking and biking trails; clinics for all ages-sailing, kayaking, tennis & golf; Relax with Yoga on the waterfront;

Winter brings (equipment rentals available) cross country skiing on groomed trails through the 400 scenic acres; back country skiing on the Jack Rabbit trail system extending miles through the mountains; snowshoeing; ice skating and tubing; walking distance to two restaurants including the famous Lake Placid Lodge for fine dining or pub dining;

The resort is a short drive to the Olympic Village of Lake Placid for exploring the Olympic venues, shopping and restaurants; Ski or Mountain Bike Whiteface Mountain, 9 miles from the Village; Cross Country Ski at the Olympic Complex/Mt. Vanhoevenberg; go for a Bobsled or Luge run; Ice Skate on the Olympic Oval; Lake Placid has it all.
