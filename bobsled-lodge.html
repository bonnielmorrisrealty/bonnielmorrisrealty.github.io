- Bobsled Lodge -

Lake Placid Vacation Rental

Bobsled Lodge is a meticulously restored Adirondack Retreat that offers spectacular vistas of the 1.5-mile-long Olympic Bobsled Run, Mount Van Hoevenberg, the High Peaks range and surrounded by forested wilderness lands. Situated on 5 acres of total privacy that is perfect for large groups, bonfires, cookouts and watching the stars. Ample sleeping capacity for large groups with beds for 18 and if you have a larger group, ask your host about coordinating the use of tents, RV’s along with catering services.

The main lodge features five bedrooms, master and one guest bedroom with ensuite baths along with two additional shared bathrooms.

The first floor has an open floor plan that is perfect for entertaining. A great room with vaulted ceilings, stone gas fireplace, dining/sitting area, fully equipped ‘Chef’ kitchen with Quartz counter tops and stainless-steel appliances. First floor also includes two bedrooms with shared full bath and glass doors leading to a fantastic deck.

A finished lower level has an open floor plan that includes a queen bed, sitting area, utility room, full bath, another fully equipped kitchen and a game room with ping pong table and bar.

Adjacent to the Main Lodge is the two-car garage with a beautifully finished upstairs that includes a king bedroom, living, dining and kitchen area, a pull-out sofa bed and covered balcony overlooking the vistas.

The Adirondacks offer so many opportunities to experience the great outdoors. Numerous nearby lakes and rivers provide our guests with swimming, rafting, boating, kayaking, SUP, canoeing, and fishing. The mountains offer hiking, rock climbing, ice climbing, and of course, winter skiing. Additionally, there are opportunities for horseback riding, snow shoeing, cross country skiing and summer hiking and camping. There are many guide services and ski shops that can help you plan your outdoor events and can rent you all the equipment you need. There is also plenty to do if you are not the "outdoorsy" type: stores, gift shops, theaters, art galleries, bookstores, yarn shops, farm markets, Olympic Museums, and stunning road trip opportunities. Inside Bobsled Lodge you will find a notebook that will give you some of my personal suggestions for hiking, restaurants exciting things to do.
