- Bobsled Lodge -

Lake Placid Residential

Bobsled Lodge is a meticulously restored Adirondack Retreat that offers spectacular vistas of the 1.5-mile-long Olympic Bobsled Run, Mount Van Hoevenberg, the High Peaks range and surrounded by forested wilderness lands. Situated on 5 acres of total privacy that is perfect for large groups, bonfires, cookouts and watching the stars. Ample sleeping capacity for large groups with beds for 12.

The main lodge features five bedrooms, master and one guest bedroom with ensuite baths along with two additional shared bathrooms.

The first floor has an open floor plan that is perfect for entertaining. A great room with vaulted ceilings, stone gas fireplace, dining/sitting area, fully equipped ‘Chef’ kitchen with Quartz counter tops and stainless-steel appliances. First floor also includes two bedrooms with shared full bath and glass doors leading to a fantastic deck.

A finished lower level has an open floor plan that includes a queen bed, sitting area, utility room, full bath, another fully equipped kitchen and a game room with ping pong table and bar.

A few hundred feet away is the detached two-car garage with a second-floor guest suite offering a fully equipped kitchen, dining/living area, king bedroom, and gorgeous balconies.

This property is being offered with most of the furnishings included.
