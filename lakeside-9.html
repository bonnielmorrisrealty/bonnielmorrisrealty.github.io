- Lakeside #9 -

Whiteface Club & Resort

Lakeside #9 is a rare opportunity to own a lower, end unit directly on the waterfront of Whiteface Club & Resort. Situated on the far end of the complex this two bedroom, two bath unit offers fantastic views of Lake Placid lake, Whiteface Mountain and the surrounding High Peaks.

The open floor plan consists of the living and dining area with a wood burning stone fireplace, open kitchen and glass doors leading to a brick patio and yard with pathways leading to the swimming beach, marina and waterfront restaurant.

Monthly common fees give the owner's a five person membership to the resort for golf, tennis, use of the beach, boat slips rental are an additional fee, hiking & biking trails along with adjoining Jackrabbit trail system and Peninsula trail system; Winter brings groomed cross country trails on the golf course and non-groomed trails on State owned lands that adjoin the Whiteface Club; also included is maintenance of the exterior of the building and the grounds every day throughout the year.

Excellent opportunity for seasonal rental/use with historic rental rates of $2,300 per week; Being sold turnkey-completely furnished.
