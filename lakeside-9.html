- Lakeside #9 -

Whiteface Club & Resort

Lakeside 9 is a unique offering for Whiteface Club Resort with direct lake access and an asking price of under $400,000.

This end unit is situated on the lower level of the three story building with two patios, one in front that overlooks the lake and mountains and walks out to beautifully landscaped and manicured lawns with a pathway to the swimming beach, marina, boat slips and lake front restaurant.

The side entrance is a covered patio with ample space for a small exterior storage unit or firewood area; Interior of the condo offers a small foyer with closet and tiled floor, open floor plan includes living room with wood burning stone fireplace, dining area with glass doors leading to the front patio, and kitchen; The unit features two bedrooms, each with large closet and two full baths;

The HOA fees include a five person membership to the Resort with full use of WFC's private beach with marina and boat slip’s (additional fee), 18 hole Championship Golf Course rated 11th in New York State and 4th for historic courses, 6 beautiful clay tennis courts, cross country skiing & snowshoeing on groomed trails, hiking and biking trails on the adjoining State owned lands and Jackrabbit trail system.

There are two restaurants at the Resort, one at the lake front/marina and the other at the golf course. Within a 3 minute walk to the legendary Lake Placid Lodge for fine dining or pub dining at Maggie’s Pub. The Whiteface Club and Resort is a 5 minute drive from downtown Lake Placid or a leisurely hike or ski on the Jackrabbit Trail through the forest along the banks of Lake Placid lake.

Lake Placid is a year round destination town with hundreds of hiking trails, easy access to all 46 high peaks, world famous trout fishing in the Ausable River, Olympic venues for the public’s use, numerous diverse restaurants and shopping.

Whiteface Mountain is located 9 miles away and is open year round with seasonal activities, mountain biking in the summer months, downhill skiing and snowboarding in the winter months. Whiteface Mountain was voted the number one ski area in the east. As a site for Ironman USA in July the town is training friendly, runners, cyclists and swimmers are abundant, so why not join in.

Also as an Olympic training site both summer and winter there are plenty of athletic training and competitions to enjoy…Soccer, Rugby, Lacrosse, Equestrian, Ice Hockey, kayaking, rowing, mountain biking, fly fishing, sailing, boating… the list is long, with plenty to see and do year round.
