- Dreaming Tree Loj -

Lake Placid Residential

Dreaming Tree Loj gives guest the feeling of the Adirondack Mountains as soon as you walk in the door. Open floor plan with vaulted ceilings, stone fireplace(gas), dining for 6-8, granite counter tops, fully equipped kitchen enhanced with Birch Bark, stainless steel appliances; 1/2 bath off great room; first floor bedroom with bunk twin over double; Barked on log hand crafted stairway and railing leading upstairs to a master bedroom with queen bed, guest bedroom with a double bed and full bath with jetted tub, solid wood counter, tiled shower and skylight. AC in both upstairs bedrooms; Sono speakers-play music from your phone; pack N play and high chair available;

Situated one block up from Main Street and a close walk to a park and down the hill to Main. There is a free trolley that runs past the unit and travels around Lake Placid Village;(summer months only) One car parking with 24 hour municipal parking available off Main Street for additional cars.

The Adirondack Mountains offer many opportunities for those who love the outdoors in all seasons. This vast wilderness with hiking trails, hundreds of miles of waterways allow guest to hike, swim, kayak, canoe, whitewater rafting, SUP and fishing. And of course in the winter we can provide you with ice mountains to climb, cross country skiing, snowshoeing and down hill skiing on Whiteface Mountain.

Lake Placid Village, just a 5 minute walk, offers shopping, restaurants, night life, Olympic Museums, markets and Mirror Lake for swimming & winter sports.

This condo is in the perfect location to enjoy all Lake Placid has to offer.
