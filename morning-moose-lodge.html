Lake Placid Residential

Welcome to Morning Moose, Unit #45 Townhome; Situated in one of the best vacation locations in Lake Placid within a 2 block stroll to Main Street Lake Placid and Mirror Lake;

This Adirondack style home sleeps 6-10 people and offers an open floor plan perfect for entertaining. First floor consists of a fully equipped kitchen, dining area for 8, great room with vaulted ceilings and queen pull out sofa bed, stone gas fireplace, laundry room and a full bathroom with jetted tub;

The second floor consists of a queen bedroom, a guest bedroom with two double beds, a full bathroom and an open loft area with queen pull out sofa bed and private balcony;

Morning Moose is an end unit allowing for ample privacy and a patio with gas grill; Additional amenities: Four new smart TV’s; central AC; Ample parking; Non-smoking; well behaved pets are allowed with an additional pet fee of $100.00; All linens and basic household items included;

Mirror Lake, a public beach area, tennis courts, shops, restaurants, Olympic Center, Olympic Oval and many Olympic venues are all within a 5 minute walk; Whiteface Mountain is 9 miles from Lake Placid with shuttle buses running every 45 minutes; Free Village Shuttle bus runs throughout the day; Horse Show grounds-2 miles; Olympic Ski Jumping Complex-2 miles;

Upon checking into your home, you will find your ‘Welcome’ notebook which offers useful information for your stay; emergency numbers, things to do & see, close hiking/skiing trails, recommended restaurants and shopping, along with ‘check out’ requirements.

Have a great stay!
